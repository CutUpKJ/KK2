Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 5 Mins
  • Serves: 1 Person
Ingredients Quantity
Chedder Cheese, Butter, Bread 2 Slices of Chedder Cheese, One slab of butter, 2 pieces of bread.

  • Step 1: Open up the bread.
  • Step 2:Put your cheese on bread and close the breads.
  • Step 3:Put butter in pan until melted.
  • Step 4:Place bread with cheese in pan until bread is golden brown

    Cereal

    • Time: 1 Min
    • Serves: 1 Person
    Ingredients Quantity
    Cornflakes and milk Bowl full of cornflakes and bowl full of milk.

  • Step 1:Pour your cornflakes into bowl.
  • Step 2:Pour milk on the cornflakes.
  • Step 3:Grab a spoon.
  • Step 4:Enjoy!

    • Scrambled Eggs

    • Time: 3 Min
    • Serves: 1 Person
    Ingredients Quantity
    Eggs, Butter, Salt, Pepper 2 Eggs, Slab of butter, Pinch of Salt an Pepper

  • Step 1:Put butter into hot pan.
  • Step 2:Crack two eggs into pan.
  • Step 3:Use wooden spoon to move the eggs around with and scramble them.
  • Step 4:Sprinkle salt and pepper onto the cooked eggs.
